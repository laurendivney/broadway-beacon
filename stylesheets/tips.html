GETTING READY FOR YOUR VISIT


*Buying tickets ahead of time could be the safe choice, but also the expensive choice. You have to pay for the ticket at a full price and you have to pay for the fee. My advice is to wait and buy the tickets the day of.


*Look up all the shows you want to see and find out where the theatre is. This will help you with your surroundings so you won't be unprepared.


CHEAP TICKETS


*Tons of options are avaliable when looking for cheap tickets.

-SRO: Standing room only: ask for standing room only tickets the day of the performance at the box office. Make sure to arrive before or once the box office opens since they only have a limited number of SRO tickets.

-online lottery: the majority of shows have online lottery. To see if your show has it, click
